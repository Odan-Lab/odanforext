How Odan Works

Discover the revolutionary process of tokenizing real estate assets for accessible, borderless investment opportunities.

1

Property Selection

Our team carefully selects high-value real estate properties with strong growth potential across global markets.

2

Legal Structuring

Each property is legally structured to enable fractional ownership while complying with local regulations.

3

Tokenization

The property is divided into digital tokens on the OdanChain, each representing a share of ownership.

4

Investor Access

Tokens are made available to investors through our secure platform, with options for different investment amounts.

5

Secondary Market

Investors can trade their property tokens on our decentralized exchange for liquidity.

6

Revenue Distribution

Rental income and capital gains are automatically distributed to token holders via smart contracts.
