The ODAN Token Economy

ODAN tokens serve as the lifeblood of our platform, creating a circular economy that benefits all participants:

  • Medium of Exchange: Used for all platform transactions
  • Governance: Token holders vote on platform decisions
  • Rewards: Earn tokens through participation and referrals
  • Discounts: Reduced fees when using tokens for transactions
